Two tabs & collapsible cards

The panel has two tabs at the top: Chat Tools (everything below) and Creator Tools (lorebook tools, see that tab's own help button).

Every section is a collapsible card: click a card's header to fold it away and keep the panel tidy. Your layout is remembered. You can also drag the panel by its top bar, or click the – button to shrink it down to just the sun icon.

Chat Tools features
📊
Session Stats
on by default

Keeps a live count of your messages, the bot's messages, and the total, plus how many times you have clicked Regenerate. Can be toggled off independently from the Nexus reminder.

🔮
Nexus Reminder
on by default

Counts messages sent since you last opened the Nexus. Not mandatory at all, but if you like keeping tabs on it and pruning unnecessary nodes when it gets high, it's a handy reference to have.

Under 20: all good 20 to 40: worth a check Over 40: check Nexus now
💡Checking your Nexus regularly and pruning unnecessary details or duplicated nodes can improve longform roleplay health!
🔁
Save Regenerations
on by default

When you click Regenerate, the toolkit saves the previous reply before it disappears. Browse all saved versions in the panel with the arrow buttons.

Found one you prefer? Click Use this reply and the toolkit updates the message so the AI treats it as the real reply when you send next. Discard clears the saved list if you want to start fresh without sending.

💡Saved replies clear automatically when you send your next message.
✏️
User Input Recovery
on by default

As you type, the toolkit saves what you have written. If the page crashes, times out, or logs you out, your unsent text will not be lost.

The toolkit also saves your last 5 sent messages. This is useful when the Stop button accidentally deletes your most recent message. Browse and restore any of them from the panel.

🛑
Auto-Refresh on Stop
on by default

Stopping a generation mid-way can cause double messages, missing messages, or broken formatting. With this on, a 3-second countdown appears after hitting Stop. Cancel if you need to copy something first.

🧠
Delete Thinking Button

Nyx and Athena models only. These models show their internal reasoning in a collapsible box before the final reply. Once you have read it, a Remove thinking button appears at the top of the message to delete it permanently.

⬇️
Download & scroll buttons

There are three buttons at the bottom of Chat Tools:

↑ Scroll to first message: slowly scrolls up to load every older message in the chat. When it finishes it asks you to confirm it really reached the first message (if not, just scroll again).

⬇ Download chat (.txt): saves the whole conversation as a plain text file, with the character's name on each bot message. If you haven't scrolled up yet, it offers to do that first so nothing is missed.

↓ Back to bottom: jumps you straight back down to the latest message.

💡Scroll to first message is also a handy way to jump to the very start of a long chat. Just scroll up, then close the confirmation.
🔄Didn't reach the first message? Refresh the page and try again. If it still won't reach the start after a couple of tries, please let Sunny know!
